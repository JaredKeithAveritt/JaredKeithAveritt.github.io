Experienced PhD candidate specializing in atomic scale physics of 2D materials, with a strong passion for bridging the theory-experiment gap through computational methodologies. My current research focuses on developing numerical methods to analyze the forcefield interactions between pyrene derivatives and a 2D interface, enabling high throughput configurational space exploration. By leveraging this expertise, I aspire to contribute to the simulation and screening of cutting-edge sensing devices utilizing 2D materials.
